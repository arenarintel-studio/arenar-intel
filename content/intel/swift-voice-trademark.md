Taylor Swift has filed trademark applications in the United States to protect her voice and image amid growing concerns over artificial intelligence impersonations.

The filings include one application using a photo of Swift on stage during her Eras Tour, along with two audio clips of her introducing herself during album promotions.

The move follows increasing use of AI-generated content resembling public figures, including manipulated images and fake endorsements.

According to the filing details, the trademarks could allow Swift to challenge both direct copies and AI-generated imitations that are considered confusingly similar under US law.
