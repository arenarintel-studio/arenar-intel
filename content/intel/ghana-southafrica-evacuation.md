Ghana has received its first group of citizens evacuated from South Africa following reports of anti immigrant tensions and attacks on foreign nationals.

The flight, carrying around 300 Ghanaians, arrived in Accra where authorities provided reception support and initial reintegration assistance.

Officials said the evacuation was coordinated in response to safety concerns affecting foreign nationals in parts of South Africa.

Upon arrival, returnees were given transport assistance and guidance on reintegration support services.
