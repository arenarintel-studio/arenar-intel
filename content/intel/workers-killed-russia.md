A Ukrainian drone strike killed three utility workers in the Russia controlled part of the Donetsk region in eastern Ukraine, according to local authorities.

Officials said the workers were part of a repair crew for a water supply company and were travelling in a vehicle when it was struck.

A fourth person was seriously injured in the incident, according to the same reports.

Local officials also reported additional injuries in separate incidents across parts of the region under Russian control.

The area has remained a frequent site of fighting between Russian and Ukrainian forces since the start of the war.
