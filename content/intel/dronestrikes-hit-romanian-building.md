A drone struck a residential building in the city of Galati, Romania, near the Ukraine border, injuring two people, according to Romania’s emergency inspection authority.

Authorities said the drone hit a 10th floor apartment, causing a fire in the building.

Firefighters responded to the scene and brought the fire under control after evacuating around 70 residents.

The emergency services reported that the drone’s explosive payload detonated on impact, though no further technical details were provided.

In a separate incident, Romanian local authorities reported that another drone without an explosive charge was found in Maramures county, and the area was secured while investigations continued.

Authorities are also examining related drone activity reported across the border in Ukraine’s Odesa region, where drone attacks were recorded in the early hours of Friday.
