GCB Bank has been named Best Regional Bank in West Africa at the 20th African Banker Awards, held in Brazzaville on the sidelines of the African Development Bank Annual Meetings.

The award places Ghana’s largest locally owned bank among leading financial institutions recognised for performance across innovation, infrastructure financing, and inclusive banking initiatives.

The ceremony brought together senior African financial and political leaders, including representatives of Congo’s government and the President of the African Development Bank.

Ecobank was named Bank of the Year at the event, while other awards highlighted achievements in development finance, infrastructure investment, and gender-focused banking initiatives across the continent.

Organisers described the awards as a benchmark for excellence in African banking, recognising institutions driving growth and financial inclusion across multiple markets.

GCB Bank’s recognition adds to growing visibility for Ghana’s banking sector within regional financial systems.
