The US military has denied claims that an American aircraft was shot down near Bushehr in Iran.

The denial follows a report from Iranian state television that said a US aircraft had been destroyed in the Jam governorate in Bushehr province, citing a local governor.

US military officials rejected the claim, stating that no US aircraft were shot down in the area.

No additional details were provided regarding the Iranian report or the circumstances surrounding the claim.
