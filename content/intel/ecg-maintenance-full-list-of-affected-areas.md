The Electricity Company of Ghana (ECG) has announced planned maintenance on Tuesday, 7th July, along with emergency maintenance on Monday, 6th July, as part of efforts to improve service delivery. The works affect the Ashanti, Central, and Accra West regions, while engineers separately work to fix network faults in the Tema Region. Below is the full list of areas and times affected.

## Ashanti Region — Tuesday, 7 July (9:00am–3:00/4:00pm)
Kumawu, Oyoko, Banko, Akrofonso, Asamang, Atwea, Paah, Pakyi No.1 and 2, Anwiankwanta, Kentinkrono, Tech Police Station, Ayigya Cemetery, Royal Foams, Okess School, Bremang UGC, Atimatim, Pankrono Zongo, Konongo Low Cost, Juansa, Hwediem, Agogo, Nwamase, Apire, Ampatia, Darko, Trede, Ampabame, Mpatasie, Bekwame, Semanhyiakrom, Sabin Akrofrom, Kyekyewere, Nkawie, Bebu, Mpasatia, Toase, Onwe, Asienimpong, Kwaso, Yam Market, Dote Nima, parts of Buokrom Junction, and surrounding areas.

## Central Region — Tuesday, 7 July (9:00am–4:00pm)
Kpomotey, Adam Nana, Ashalaja, Domeabra, Kwame Anum, Hobor, VRA, Nurses Quarters, Denchira, Tomefa, Police Farm, and surrounding areas.

## Accra West Region — Tuesday, 7 July (9:00am–5:00pm)
Mayera, Dunyo, Manchie, Satellite, Korleman, ATU, Nsufa, NIC, Anyaa Deeper Life, Anyaa Market, Upper Weija, Old Weija, Oblogo, Topbase, and surrounding areas.

## Accra West Region — Emergency Maintenance, Monday, 6 July (9:00am–5:00pm)
ABC, Down, Saint John, SHS, Pentecost University, Israel Park, Israel Filling Station, Ayigbe Junction, Shiabu, Beach Road, Asofan, White House, Poultry Farm, Amelia, Ayawaso Hills, and surrounding areas.

## Tema Region — Ongoing Faults
Customers in the Tema Region continue to experience unplanned outages due to network faults. Affected areas include Baatsonaa, Hisense, K Ofori Apartment, Police Station, and surrounding areas. ECG says engineers have been deployed to fix the faults and restore supply as quickly as possible.
