Arenar Editorial Team

Arenar Intelligence is produced by a small editorial team committed to fast, verified, and source-driven reporting. Below is more about the founder and the editorial desk responsible for what you read.

Founder & Editor

Seth Anokye Amoyaw is the founder and editor of Arenar Intelligence. Based in Accra, Ghana, he leads editorial direction and contributes original reporting and analysis covering geopolitics, markets, technology, and Africa.

Seth founded Arenar Intelligence to provide modern readers with news that is fast, precise, and verified.

The Intel Desk

The Intel Desk is the collective editorial staff of Arenar Intelligence, responsible for synthesizing, verifying, and publishing news drawn from multiple sources.

The desk handles general news coverage where reporting is compiled from wire services, official statements, public datasets, and other verifiable references. All articles are reviewed for accuracy and clarity before publication.

The Intel Desk operates under the editorial direction of founder Seth Anokye Amoyaw from Arenar's editorial base in Accra, Ghana.
